	\section{Electric Potential}
		Electrostatic forces are \textbf{conservative}, so \(W_C = -\Delta U\). It can then be seen that
			\[
				\Delta U = - \int_C \vec{F} \cdot \dd{\vec{r}} 
						= -q\int_C \vec{E} \cdot \dd{\vec{r}}
			\]
